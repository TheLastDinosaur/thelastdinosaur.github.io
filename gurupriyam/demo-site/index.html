ABOUT US

“WE BELIEVE GREAT THINGS WILL HAPPEN ONLY WHEN ONE TRIES”

  • Founded in 2015
  • Playschool mindset exploring new boundaries
  • Diverse minds from across India, Netherlands, Canada, Dubai and Malaysia
  • Collaboration with best minds across the globe

We specialize in the art of Next gen story telling through Use of design, art and technology

  • Creative Technology Consulting
  • Pioneer in new media technologies
  • Multidisciplinary Mixed Multimedia Projects
  • Rapid prototyping
  • Interaction design
  • Product incubation
  • Turnkey projects on Digital Experience Centers, Digital
  • Museums and art installations
  • Custom workshops on digital innovation
  • Musical Fountains, Laser Show , Light & Sound Show,
  • Projection Mapping
  • AR, VR, MR

We have a unique organization Structure

  • Nimble approach
  • Flat organization
  • Malleable and buoyant
  • Project ownership

We invest continuously in our Future
